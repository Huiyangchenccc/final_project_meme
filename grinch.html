Grinch Meme

Story Behind Grinch

The GIF you shared comes from the classic 1966 animated television special How the Grinch Stole Christmas!, which was directed by Chuck Jones and based on Dr. Seuss's 1957 book. The specific smirking expression of the Grinch in the animation—often referred to as the "Grinch grin"—has become a widely used meme.

Created Date and Source

While the animation is from 1966, the meme itself became popular much later—around the early 2010s—thanks to the rise of reaction GIFs and meme culture on platforms like Tumblr, Reddit, and Twitter.

Major Achievements

4.2 million views on TikTok within 10 days of its release in December 2024
